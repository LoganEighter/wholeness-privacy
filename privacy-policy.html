Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Wholeness Work is a personal meditation and inner process app. This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.

No data collection

Wholeness Work does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. There is no account system, no login, and no connection to the internet while you use it.

Data stored on your device

Session notes, history, and settings are saved locally on your device using standard browser storage. That data stays on your device and is only accessible to you.

Audio files

All audio in the app, including voice guidance, binaural beats, and background music, is stored locally. The app never records audio from your device.

Third-party services

This app does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any third-party services of any kind. Nothing is shared externally.

Children's privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If anything here changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date at the top.
